About

The fire is a difficult 3D platformer in a world that grows around you.

Gameplay

You attempt to unravel your past while traversing through low-poly terrain

As you progress through the game the music will adapt to the difficulty of the stages

Collect floating orbs to expand the size of the levels

Where?

The game is currently on Steam and Itch.io with demos on Itch and Gamejolt
